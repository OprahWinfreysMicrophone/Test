How We Help

Providing essential services to those who need them most

🍲

Nutrition & Meals

Hot meals, meal delivery, and nutrition assistance for seniors and families facing food insecurity.

🚘

Transportation

Rides to medical appointments, grocery stores, and community events for those who need it.

🤝

Care Coordination

Connecting individuals with health services, social workers, and community resources.

👪

Family Support

Counseling, parenting resources, and emergency assistance for families in need.

📚

Education & Outreach

Workshops, classes, and information sessions to empower our community members.

💓

Social Connection

Activities, groups, and events to reduce isolation and build community bonds.
